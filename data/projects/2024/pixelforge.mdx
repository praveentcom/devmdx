## What It Does

PixelForge brings AI image generation to iOS and macOS with a focus on privacy. Your prompts and images never leave your device.

## Features

- Text-to-image generation
- Image editing and enhancement
- iCloud sync across devices
- Supports multiple AI models

## Tech Stack

Built with Swift and SwiftUI. Uses Core ML for on-device inference and CloudKit for sync.
